How it works

Onboard once. Train day by day. Sync when you want.

Onboard

Set a goal — 5K, 10K, Half, Marathon, or general fitness — and your equipment level.

Plan

Get a multi-week calendar (4–52 weeks) with runs and strength/mobility on one schedule.

Today

Open today’s session: structured segments with pace, effort, and how-to — not just “run X km.”

Sync

Optionally import watch activities through Health Connect and match them to planned sessions.

Built for runners who lift

Practical coaching on device — beginner to advanced. No cloud coaching account required.

Dual coach

Race/run plan and strength/mobility on one calendar so both stay in sync with your week.

Structured runs

Segments with pace, effort, and clear instructions — not a bare distance target.

Pace from your 5K

Targets and pace charts from your estimate — using Health Connect speed when available.

Offline-first privacy

Sync & Garmin — the honest story

Watch data can reach BoddyFit. Maps and Garmin cloud downloads cannot.

Watch → Garmin Connect → Health Connect → BoddyFit

Garmin Connect disclaimer

BoddyFit does not connect directly to Garmin Connect and cannot download GPS maps or activity files from Garmin’s cloud.

Watch activities reach BoddyFit only through Android Health Connect: Garmin Connect writes what it chooses to share; BoddyFit reads that data on your phone. Distance, time, heart rate, and pace samples may appear; GPS route maps are not shown.
