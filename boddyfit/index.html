Sync & Garmin — the honest story

Watch data can reach BoddyFit. Maps and Garmin cloud downloads cannot.

Watch → Garmin Connect → Health Connect → BoddyFit

Garmin Connect disclaimer

BoddyFit does not connect directly to Garmin Connect and cannot download GPS maps or activity files from Garmin’s cloud.

Watch activities reach BoddyFit only through Android Health Connect: Garmin Connect writes what it chooses to share; BoddyFit reads that data on your phone. Distance, time, heart rate, and pace samples may appear; GPS route maps are not shown.

Calendar can export weekly run files (FIT) for your own watch workflow — separate from Health Connect imports.
