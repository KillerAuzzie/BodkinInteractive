FAQs

Quick answers for the questions we hear most often.

How do I get BoddyFit?

BoddyFit is an Android app from Bodkin Interactive. Use the contact form (select BoddyFit) or email us for access / Play Store updates. Product overview: boddyfit/.

Does BoddyFit need an account or internet?

No cloud account is required. Plans and history stay on your phone. The app is offline-first — you only need connectivity for optional things like Health Connect sync or opening linked coaching videos.

Does BoddyFit connect directly to Garmin Connect?

No. BoddyFit does not log into Garmin’s cloud or download GPS maps from Garmin. Watch activities reach BoddyFit only if Garmin Connect (or another app) writes them to Android Health Connect and you grant BoddyFit permission to read them.

Why don’t I see my run / HR / map after sync?

Check that Garmin Connect (or your watch app) is writing to Health Connect, then that BoddyFit has Health Connect permission. Distance, time, heart rate, and pace samples may appear when available; GPS route maps are not shown in BoddyFit.

What are FIT exports for?

You can export planned runs as Garmin .fit files on your device for your own watch workflow. That is separate from Health Connect imports and does not upload files to Bodkin Interactive.

How do I delete my data?

There is no cloud account to wipe. Delete content in-app where available, clear BoddyFit storage in Android Settings → Apps → BoddyFit → Storage, or uninstall the app. Revoke Health Connect permissions if you no longer want imports. Full details: Privacy policy.

Is BoddyFit medical advice?

No. BoddyFit provides general fitness coaching for information only. It is not a medical device. See the Terms of use before starting or changing a training program.
