About

Bodkin Interactive is an independent game studio dedicated to crafting immersive worlds and unique player experiences. Our team is passionate about bringing fresh, engaging gameplay to life.

Fields Of Renown is an immersive medieval sandbox RPG set in 13th-century Gwynedd, Wales, where survival, strategy, and skill define your journey. As a lone wanderer or a thriving merchant, you'll fish, farm, trade, and fight to carve out your legacy in a world shaped by seasons, economy, and conflict. Master your craft, forge alliances, and adapt to the ever-changing landscape as you build your renown in a land filled with opportunity‚Äîand danger.
Will you become a respected landowner, a feared warrior, or a legendary explorer?

The choice is yours.
