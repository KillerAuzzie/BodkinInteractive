Fields of Renown

A medieval sandbox RPG set in 13th-century Gwynedd — fish, farm, trade, and fight as you carve renown from a living Welsh landscape.

Join our Discord Watch development

In development · Unreal Engine 5 · Bodkin Interactive

The world

Survival, strategy, and skill in a land shaped by seasons, economy, and conflict.

As a lone wanderer or a thriving merchant, you build a life in northwest Wales. Master crafts, forge alliances, and adapt as weather and markets shift — will you become a respected landowner, a feared warrior, or a legendary explorer?

The choice is yours.

Gameplay pillars

Systems that make the sandbox feel lived-in — not checklist busywork.

  • Fishing Cast into Welsh rivers and lakes as a core early craft.
  • Economy Dynamic trading and markets that reward attention.
  • Combat Medieval conflict where timing and tactics matter.
  • Farming Cultivate land and harvest with the turning year.
  • Seasons Weather and seasons that change how you play.
  • Skills Level crafts and abilities as your renown grows.

Development

Building in the open — foundation first, then the systems that make Wales feel alive.

Current focus

Unreal Engine 5 with C++ and Blueprints — laying foundations for the fishing mechanics prototype.

  1. Phase 1 · Foundation

    Engine setup, core design, and community building.

    Q4 2025 – mid 2026

  2. Phase 2 · Prototype

    Movement, fishing, and early environment systems.

    H2 2026

  3. Phase 3 · Alpha

    Combat, economy, and closed community testing.

    2027

  4. Phase 4 · Beta

    Full feature set, balancing, and open beta.

    2027+
