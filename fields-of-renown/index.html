Fields of Renown

A medieval sandbox RPG set in 13th-century Gwynedd — survival, craft, trade, and conflict in a land shaped by seasons.

Join our Discord Watch development

In early development · Unreal Engine 5 · by Bodkin Interactive

The world

North Wales, thirteenth century. Opportunity and danger share the same road.

As a lone wanderer or a rising merchant, you fish, farm, trade, and fight to carve out your place. Seasons shift the land. Markets move. Alliances form and break. Build renown on your terms — landowner, warrior, explorer, or something in between.

Gameplay pillars

Systems that shape everyday life in Gwynedd.

  • Fishing Welsh rivers and lakes — food, trade, and quiet hours between storms.
  • Economy Dynamic trading and markets that reward attention and risk.
  • Combat Medieval conflict that asks for timing, judgment, and steel.
  • Farming Cultivate holdings, harvest through the year, and feed what you build.
  • Seasons Weather and seasons that change travel, work, and survival.
  • Skills Level crafts and abilities as your legend takes shape.
