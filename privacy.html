Privacy & data

How BoddyFit handles your training data on this device. Effective July 19, 2026.

Garmin Connect disclaimer

BoddyFit does not connect directly to Garmin Connect and cannot download GPS maps or activity files from Garmin’s cloud.

Watch activities reach BoddyFit only through Android Health Connect: Garmin Connect writes what it chooses to share; BoddyFit reads that data on your phone. Distance, time, heart rate, pace samples, and similar metrics may appear; GPS route maps are not shown.

Who we are

BoddyFit is provided by Bodkin Interactive (bodkininteractive.com). This policy describes how the app handles information on your device.

Data we store

Training plans, workouts, runs, preferences, reminder settings, and related coaching data are stored locally on your device (IndexedDB / app storage). BoddyFit is designed to work offline-first without uploading your training history to Bodkin Interactive servers for coaching.

Health Connect permissions

On Android, you may grant BoddyFit permission to read workout data from Health Connect (for example exercise sessions, distance, heart rate, speed, steps, calories, and power when available). We use these permissions only to import and display your activities in the app and to help match them to your plan. We do not request GPS exercise routes for map display.

You control these grants in Android / Health Connect.

Notifications

Optional daily training reminders use local notifications on your device. You can enable or disable them in Settings. Notification permission is used only for those reminders.

Analytics & advertising

BoddyFit does not include third-party advertising SDKs or sell your personal training data. If this changes in a future version, we will update this policy and the in-app effective date.

Retention & deletion

Data remains on your device until you clear app storage, uninstall the app, or delete content within the app (where available). Uninstalling removes local app data from that device.

Children

BoddyFit is intended for adults and teens who train with appropriate supervision. It is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

Third parties

Garmin Connect and Health Connect are separate products from Google / Garmin. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. BoddyFit only receives data those systems expose to installed apps you have authorized.

Garmin, Garmin Connect, Google, Android, and Health Connect are trademarks of their respective owners. BoddyFit is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Garmin Ltd. or Google LLC.

Your choices

You may revoke Health Connect permissions in system settings, disable reminders in the app, and uninstall the app at any time. Related: Terms of use · Legal & copyright.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date shown in the app.
